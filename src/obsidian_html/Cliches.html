The other space opera stories that this is similar to is Star Trek and Firefly. Not to mention Star Wars. The space ships in the novel closely resemble the RPG Spell Jammer. So to the extent that I incorporate elements from each of these stories they need to be dissimilar enough that they aren't the same thing.
- A planet is blown up in Star Wars, and also in this case. However, the Great Pluto Disaster isn't a primary story arc and it doesn't have anything to do with a princess.
- In the Expanse a group of bandits steal a spaceship. Here, no one steals a spaceship and goes on an adventure. Rather it's a group of people that travel across the galaxy and are in and out of spaceships, gravity wells and see several of the major events of the narrative. So there's a quest element, but none of them own a spaceship. So there's a higher element of risk in some ways for the main characters.
